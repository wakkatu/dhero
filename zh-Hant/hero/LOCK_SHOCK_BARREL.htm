Passive: For their basic attacks, Lock and Shock shoot their slingshots, dealing fantastic damage, while Barrel licks his lollipop, healing the ally with the lowest HP.

Active: The player chooses an enemy for Lock, Shock, and Barrel to kidnap. The trio runs over to an enemy in their bathtub and puts the enemy in a sack. While an enemy is in the sack, they are dealt 260k + 0.8sp damage per second. The enemy is freed from the sack after 8 seconds.

This skill cannot be evaded and the duration is not affected by tenacity.

While the battle is on auto, Lock, Shock, and Barrel will kidnap the enemy with the highest evasion.
