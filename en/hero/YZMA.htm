Yzma becomes invincible and begins mixing a potion for up to 10 seconds. She then throws the potion at the closest enemies, dealing between 203k+1.4sp and 600k+3.9sp damage depending on how long she mixed the potion. As Yzma mixes the potion, there is a greater chance of the potion backfiring and exploding in her face, which instead deals 200% of the damage to Yzma and enemies around her.

While not on auto, the player can end her mixing early by tapping her portrait. While on auto, Yzma will always mix the potion for 2 seconds, deal 270k+1.7sp damage, and it will never blow up in her face.
