Passive: Instead of using a basic attack, Sadness cowers, dodging basic attacks for 2 seconds and gaining 1 stacks of Sorrow. Sadness also gains 3 stacks of Sorrow when an ally is KO'd. Active: Sadness targets the enemy with the most remaining HP, applying 2 stacks of Fatigue to them and creating a circle of sadness around them for 8 seconds. While in the circle, all enemies are silenced and debuffs applied to them last 50% longer.
