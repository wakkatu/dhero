Madam Mim magically transforms into different animals based on the role of the enemy that last used an active skill.

Tank Role: Mim turns into a crocodile, biting the closest enemy and dealing 400k+3.5sp fantastic damage.

Damage Role: Mim turns into a tiger and pounces on the last enemy who used their active skill, dealing 353k+2.7sp normal damage.

Control or Support Role: Mim turns into a dragon and breathes fire at the farthest enemy, dealing 325k+2.2sp fantastic damage to enemies in an area.
